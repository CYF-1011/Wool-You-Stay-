作品名稱：卡在樹上的排球

作者姓名：鄭心俞

作品主題：排球

作品敘述：這是一件以樹枝和樹葉為重點的羊毛氈。這背後還有一個有趣的故事，在體育課上，Ruby 和我意外地把排球打到了一棵樹上。即使下了兩天的大雨，球仍然卡在樹上。
This artwork is a wool felt featurng tree branches and leaves. with a vibrant volleyball as a focal point. And there is an interesting story behind it, during PE class, Ruby and I accidentally hit the valleyball into a tree. Even after two days of heavy rain the ball remained stuck in the tree.
